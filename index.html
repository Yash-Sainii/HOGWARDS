Spell Library

Lumos
✨ Illumination Charm
Produces bright light from the wand tip. Essential for navigating dark corridors throughout Hogwarts.
Wingardium Leviosa
⬆️ Levitation Charm
Makes objects float in mid-air. Requires precise wand movement and pronunciation: "Swish and flick."
Expelliarmus
⚔️ Disarming Charm
Disarms opponents. Harry Potter's signature spell and one of the most powerful defensive charms.
Expecto Patronum
👻 Patronus Charm
Conjures a Patronus to protect against Dementors. One of the most advanced spells in wizardry.
Alohomora
🔓 Unlocking Charm
Opens locked doors and windows. Typing "alohomora" reveals hidden secrets...
Stupefy
⚡ Stunning Spell
Renders targets unconscious. The primary offensive spell used in wizard duels.
Accio
🎯 Summoning Charm
Brings objects to the caster. One of the most useful charms for retrieving items.
Obliviate
🧠 Memory Charm
Erases or modifies memories. A powerful and dangerous spell used in the wizarding world.
Severus
⚰️ Severing Charm
Cuts through almost any substance. Used for spell cutting and defensive purposes.

The Great Hall

Welcome to the majestic Great Hall of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. This magnificent chamber serves as the heart of the castle, where all students gather for meals, important announcements, and cherished memories. With its enchanted ceiling reflecting the night sky, thousands of floating candles, and the long house tables, it is one of the most iconic locations in the wizarding world.
